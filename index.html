WHATS UDAAN?

Learn more about our organization and its inspirations

UDAAN is an organizations established in the country, Nepal. We aim to provide job oppurtunities to disabled people and strengthen the motive of equality in the country. The creator chose to make this a non-profit organizations. The motto of this orgnizations is "YOU ARE MORE CAPABLE". We aim to increase the rate of employment oppurtunities in the context of disabled people by 10% in the year 2022.
